Design is a part
When designers speak of graphic design they speak of it in terms of simplifying
complexity so users do not feel overwhelmed. With the increasing complexity in
industrial design of today's society, and due to the needs of mass production where
more time is usually associated with more cost, the production methods became
more complex and with them, the way designs and their production is created.
